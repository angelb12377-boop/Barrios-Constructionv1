Who We Are

Family-Owned, Bay Area Proud

Barrios Construction 20 LLC is a family-owned and operated construction company proudly serving homeowners across the San Francisco Bay Area. We were founded with a simple mission: deliver high-quality construction work with the kind of care, honesty, and dedication that only a family business can provide.

With over 10 years of hands-on experience in residential remodeling, repairs, and home improvement, we've built a reputation that speaks for itself — one satisfied customer at a time. Our team brings expertise, attention to detail, and genuine pride in craftsmanship to every job we take on, whether it's a small repair or a full home renovation.

When you hire Barrios Construction, you're not just hiring a contractor — you're partnering with a team that treats your home with the same respect and care as their own.

Our Mission

Serving Bay Area Homeowners with Pride

The San Francisco Bay Area is our home. We live here, raise our families here, and are proud to help improve the homes of our community. Our deep roots in the Bay Area mean we understand the local architecture, building codes, and the unique challenges that come with Bay Area homes.
